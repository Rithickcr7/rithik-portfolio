A multi-sensor mobile application for water stress detection using SPAD chlorophyll readings, thermal imaging, and RGB phone camera data. Creates a unified stress assessment model from leaf temperature, chlorophyll index, and visual symptoms.

SoC-Based Variable-Rate SiC-Powered Smart Knapsack Sprayer

A smart knapsack sprayer with SoC sensors for real-time crop monitoring and automated variable-rate chemical application. SiC-powered electronics enhance battery life and energy efficiency during field operations. 🏆 Award-winning project at multiple engineering colleges.
